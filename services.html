Our landscaping work and services

At Contreras Tree and Lanscape Services, we transform outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional areas that enhance your home and lifestyle. Our expert team is dedicated to providing top-notch landscaping services tailored to meet your unique needs and preferences.

Lawn Care

A lush, green lawn is the cornerstone of any great landscape. Our comprehensive lawn care services include mowing, fertilization, aeration, and weed control to ensure your lawn stays healthy and vibrant throughout the year.

Tree and Shrub Care

Healthy trees and shrubs add beauty and value to your property. We offer expert pruning, trimming, disease treatment, and fertilization to keep your trees and shrubs in excellent condition, enhancing the overall look of your landscape.

Free Consultations

We believe that every great project starts with a great conversation. That's why we offer free consultations to discuss your vision and provide expert advice. Let us help you bring your landscaping dreams to life with a customized plan that fits your budget and timeline.
